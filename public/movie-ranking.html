My top 3 movies of all time

YOUR NAME

The movie "Your Name" (Kimi no Na wa) is a critically acclaimed Japanese animated film directed by Makoto Shinkai. Released in 2016, it tells the story of two teenagers, Taki and Mitsuha, who mysteriously ,swap bodies and embark on a journey to meet each other, blending elements of romance, fantasy, and drama. The film is renowned for its stunning animation, ,emotional depth, and intricate storytelling.

intersteller

"Interstellar," directed by Christopher Nolan, is a 2014 sci-fi epic that follows a group of astronauts who travel ,through a wormhole in search of a new habitable planet to save humanity. The film is praised for its stunning visuals, thought-provoking ,exploration of time and space, and an emotive score by Hans Zimmer.

ATTACK ON TITAN

"Attack on Titan" (Shingeki no Kyojin) is a popular Japanese anime and manga series created by Hajime Isayama. It follows the story of humanity's struggle for survival against giant humanoid creatures known as Titans,,featuring intense action, complex characters, and deep, intertwining plotlines.
